I've been mentoring pretty consistently for the last four or five years, mostly through a mix of formal programs and informal one-on-one work. At my current company I've directly mentored three junior engineers on my team through promotions, and I've also done broader mentorship through [company program name] where I've worked with mid-level engineers on technical growth and interview prep.
