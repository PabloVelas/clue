How long have you been working with distributed systems, and can you walk me through a time you had to design one from scratch?

I'd say roughly ten years now, mostly on the backend side. One I'm proud of is the reconciliation system I built at Stripe. We were running a legacy Hadoop batch job that took fourteen hours end to end on a good day, and finance couldn't get accurate ledger views until well after close of business. I designed and shipped a streaming replacement on Kafka and Flink that brought that latency down to about thirty eight minutes at p99 and cut compute cost by roughly sixty one percent.
